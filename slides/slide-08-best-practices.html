Limitations & Best Practices

  • ⚠️
    Hallucinations & Bugs: AI code is not perfect, no replacement for a developer. Always review and test the code.
  • 🔒
    Security: Be cautious when using these tools with proprietary or sensitive codebases.
  • 🧠
    Critical Thinking is Key: Use the LLM to augment your skills, not replace your understanding.
  • ✅
    Best Practice: Provide clear, specific prompts for better results.
